About Xpert

Xpert is a marketplace that brings together Service Providers and Service Seekers.

The idea behind Xpert is to give Service Providers a platform and an opportunity to showcase the expertise, skill and services they offer

Service seekers have the opportunity to be exposed to s multitude of service providers from which they can choose from to execute their projects

The idea of Xpert stemmed from my experiences as well as witnessing other's experiences. Very often it's not easy getting access to experts for our projects. We have to rely on word of mouth and recommendations. Even with this, we are limited and settle for the available options. Xpert is built to provide a platform wherein, a person in need can quickly login and search for their service of interest and choose the best.

Many Service providers do not always make the most out of their skills and expertise. They are often limited by their location and small client size. Xpert comes in as a solution. With Xpert, a service provider is able to reach a large client base and expose their services and expertise to them. Service providers have the opportunity to secure business opportunities from clients in different areas that were not previously possible.

As part of the requirements necessary to complete the ALX Software Engineering foundations and move to specialization, we the learners are expected to build a project and submit. ALX SE is 12 months software engineering program offered bny Holberton School and trains Africa's future software engineers, all over the entire continent.
